Gameplay & Quests

In those three games you explore Different environments, relics, ruins and ancient tombs. The experience provides players with many weapons and tools to defend themselves by defeating the enemy and escaping into new levels of adventure. Gamers end up avoiding traps and challenges to finish the Quest by finding the main artifact or an ancient tomb, leading them into Lara's Immortality.

Typical Quest Steps

  1. Discover a new area and solve entry puzzles.
  2. Use tools and weapons to avoid traps.
  3. Activate switches end hidden mechanisms.
  4. Collect the Main artifact and escape alive

Tips for Ps5 Players

  • Save Often - Checkpoints can be far Apart.
  • Conserve Ammo - Precision is the key.
  • Explore optional areas to find secret treasures.
